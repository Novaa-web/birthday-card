# 🎂 Birthday Card for Angry Bird

A romantic, cinematic digital birthday letter with animations, music, and multi-page navigation.

## Features ✨

- **5 Interactive Pages**: Navigate through a romantic letter experience
- **Smooth Animations**: Cinematic transitions and floating effects
- **Background Music**: Toggle background music on/off
- **Page Navigation**: Use arrow buttons, dot indicators, or keyboard arrows (← →)
- **Fully Responsive**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Beautiful Gradients**: Purple/pink romantic color scheme

## Pages 📖

1. **Title Page** - Welcome with floating hearts
2. **Letter** - The main message
3. **Memories** - Things you love about her
4. **Wishes** - Your birthday wishes
5. **Finale** - Celebration with confetti

## How to Use 🎯

- Click **Next →** button to go to the next page
- Click **← Back** button to go to the previous page
- Click the **dots** at the bottom to jump to any page
- Use **← → Arrow keys** on keyboard for navigation
- Click **🔊 Music On** button to play/pause background music
- Fully mobile-friendly!

## Customization 🎨

You can easily customize:
- Edit the messages in `index.html`
- Change colors in `styles.css` (search for `#ff69b4` for pink color)
- Replace the background music URL in `index.html`

Enjoy! ❤️
